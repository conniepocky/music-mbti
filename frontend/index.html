Discover Your MBTI with Spotify!

How it works

  • We look at the genres of your top artists and tracks.
  • Then calculate personality traits based on these genres (e.g. Introversion vs. Extraversion, Thinking vs. Feeling, etc.).
  • Your MBTI is then derived from these traits.

Note, the accuracy of this is not 100% guaranteed - in some people music taste can be nothing like their personality! This is just for fun :)
